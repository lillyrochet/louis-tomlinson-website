2022 - Faith In The Future

Faith in the Future is the second studio album by Louis Tomlinson, released on November 11, 2022.

This album marks a notable evolution in his solo career, moving away from the sound of his debut album Walls to a more energetic and live-oriented sound. Louis described this project as a response to his live performance experiences, aiming to capture the energy of his concerts in a studio format.

To support the album, Louis launched the Faith in the Future World Tour in May 2023, performing around the world, including a performance at Lollapalooza India before more than 115,000 people, his largest crowd as a solo artist.
