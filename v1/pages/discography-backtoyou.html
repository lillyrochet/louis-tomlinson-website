2017 - "Back To You"

"Back to You" is a solo single by Louis Tomlinson, featuring Bebe Rexha and Digital Farm Animals, released on July 21, 2017.

The track blends heartfelt lyrics with catchy electropop production, capturing the emotional tension of an on-again, off-again relationship.

The music video, filmed in Louis’ hometown of Doncaster, adds a personal and authentic touch, showcasing local landmarks and highlighting his roots. With over 500 million views on YouTube, "Back to You" remains one of Tomlinson’s most successful and recognizable solo songs, marking a defining moment in his post-One Direction career.

  • CelebMix Awards (2017): Won Best Collaboration
  • MPS Online Awards (2018): Won Favorite Collaboration
