2015 - Made In The AM (1D)

The album Made in the A.M. by One Direction was released on November 13, 2015, marking a turning point for the group after Zayn Malik's departure in March 2015.

It was praised for its evolution toward a more mature and introspective sound, distinguishing itself from their previous albums.

Without Zayn Malik, Made in the A.M. introduced a new dynamic within the group, with a strong pop-rock influence, along with touches of soft ballads and more experimental sounds. It was also the band's first album before their indefinite hiatus in early 2016.

  • Gold Certification in France and Japan
  • People's Choice Awards (2016): Nominated for Favorite Music Group
