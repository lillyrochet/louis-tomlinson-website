Solo Career

While One Direction left a lasting mark on pop music, Louis was ready to write his own chapter. Determined to find his own voice outside the group, he looked to create music that reflected his experiences and personality. His first solo release came later that year with "Just Hold On," a collaboration with DJ Steve Aoki. The track, released under emotional circumstances shortly after the death of his mother, became a symbol of strength and resilience. Its success marked a promising beginning, showing that Louis was ready to step out on his own.

In 2017, he followed up with "Back to You," a duet with American singer Bebe Rexha. The song combined catchy pop hooks with emotional lyrics and reached international charts, further establishing him as a solo act capable of commercial success. With these early releases, Louis laid the foundations of his solo identity, experimenting with sound while staying true to his emotional storytelling.
