2020 - Walls

Walls is the debut solo album by Louis Tomlinson, released on January 31, 2020.

Deeply inspired by Britpop and 90s rock influences such as Oasis, Arctic Monkeys, and Richard Ashcroft, Walls presents a more organic and guitar-driven sound.

Across 12 tracks, Louis explores deeply personal themes like grief, growth, nostalgia, and resilience, all while maintaining an undercurrent of hope. The album’s production stands out for its raw, honest instrumentation — a clear departure from the electronic-heavy pop of the mainstream.

Walls was warmly received by fans and praised for its authenticity, lyrical depth, and Louis’s confident artistic voice.

  • TDY Awards (2020): Won Album of the Year for Walls
