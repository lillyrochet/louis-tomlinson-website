2016 - "Just Hold On"

"Just Hold On" is the debut solo single by Louis Tomlinson, released in collaboration with American DJ Steve Aoki on December 10, 2016. This EDM-infused track marked Tomlinson's first solo endeavor following One Direction's hiatus.

The song features uplifting lyrics encouraging perseverance through hardships. Notably, Tomlinson performed "Just Hold On" live for the first time on the finale of The X Factor UK, just days after the passing of his mother, Johannah Deakin, making the performance particularly poignant.

"Just Hold On" received positive reviews for its inspirational message and marked a successful transition for Tomlinson into his solo career.
