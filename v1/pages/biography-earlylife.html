Early Life

Louis Tomlinson, whose real name is Louis Troy Austin, was born on December 24, 1991, in Doncaster, a town in South Yorkshire, England. He is the son of Johannah Poulston and Troy Austin, who separated when he was still very young. After his parents' divorce, Louis was mainly raised by his mother, and he eventually took the surname of his stepfather, Mark Tomlinson, whom he considered an important father figure.

Louis grew up in a large blended family. He has five half-sisters: Charlotte (Lottie), Félicité, Phoebe, Daisy, and Georgia, as well as a half-brother, Ernest, and a half-sister, Doris, from another of his mother’s relationships. This lively family dynamic helped shape his down-to-earth personality and his deep attachment to family values. His close bond with his siblings remained a source of strength as he navigated his career, and even after his rise to fame, family always remained at the center of his life.
