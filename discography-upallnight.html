2011 - Up All Night (1D)

Up All Night is One Direction's debut studio album, released on November 18, 2011.

This album marks the beginning of the British group's journey, which was formed during The X Factor. Blending pop, rock, and ballads, "Up All Night" quickly won over global audiences with its catchy melodies and youthful lyrics. It features hits like "What Makes You Beautiful", which skyrocketed the band to the top of international charts, as well as "Gotta Be You" and "One Thing".

This first project catapulted One Direction into stardom, establishing them as one of the most popular pop groups of the decade.

  • Certified 4× Platinum in the UK
  • MTV Europe Music Awards (2012): Won Best Pop Album
