2014 - Four (1D)

Four is the fourth studio album by One Direction, released on November 17, 2014.

This album marks a significant evolution in the band's sound, incorporating more rock influences and a greater involvement from the members in writing the songs. It features hit singles like "Steal My Girl," "Night Changes," and "Fireproof."

The album was well-received by critics and further solidified One Direction's place as a global pop powerhouse.

  • "Steal My Girl": Reached top 10 in multiple countries
  • Billboard 200 (US): Debuted at No. 1 with 387,000 copies sold in the first week
