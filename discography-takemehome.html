2012 - Take Me Home (1D)

Take Me Home is the second studio album by British-Irish boy band One Direction, released on November 9, 2012.

Building on the massive success of their debut album Up All Night, this record further solidified the band’s global pop dominance.

With catchy hooks, youthful energy, and emotional ballads, Take Me Home includes standout tracks like "Live While We’re Young", "Little Things", and "Kiss You". The album blends upbeat anthems with tender acoustic moments, showcasing the group’s evolving sound and growing confidence.

  • IFPI: One of the best-selling albums worldwide in 2012
  • Won "Top Pop Album" at the Billboard Music Awards 2013
